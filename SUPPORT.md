## Getting Started

### First Time Setup
1. **Launch MyClipVault** - Open the app from your home screen
2. **Grant Permissions** - Allow clipboard access when prompted
3. **Complete Onboarding** - Follow the welcome screens to learn about key features
4. **Start Copying** - Any text you copy will automatically be saved to your history

### Basic Usage
- **View History**: Open MyClipVault to see all your copied items
- **Copy Again**: Tap any item to copy it back to your clipboard
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items quickly
- **Filter by Type**: Tap content type icons to filter (URLs, emails, phone numbers, etc.)

## Troubleshooting

### Clipboard Not Saving

**Problem**: Items I copy aren't appearing in MyClipVault

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Permissions**: Go to Settings > MyClipVault and ensure all permissions are granted
2. **Restart the App**: Close MyClipVault completely and reopen it
3. **Check Storage**: Ensure your device has available storage space
4. **Update iOS**: Make sure you're running the latest iOS version
5. **Reinstall**: As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app (note: this will erase your local clipboard history)

### Search Not Working

**Problem**: Search isn't finding items I know I copied

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Spelling**: Verify you're spelling the search term correctly
2. **Try Partial Search**: Search for part of the word instead of the full phrase
3. **Check Filters**: Make sure you haven't applied a filter that excludes the item
4. **Refresh**: Pull down on the list to refresh

### App Crashing

**Problem**: MyClipVault crashes or freezes

**Solutions**:
1. **Force Close**: Swipe up from the app switcher to force close, then reopen
2. **Restart Device**: Turn your iPhone/iPad off and on again
3. **Update App**: Check the App Store for the latest version
4. **Clear Cache**: If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app
5. **Report**: Contact support with your device model and iOS version

### Purchase Not Restoring

**Problem**: "Restore Purchase" isn't working

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify Apple ID**: Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase
2. **Check Internet**: Ensure you have an active internet connection
3. **Wait and Retry**: Sometimes it takes a moment; wait 30 seconds and try again
4. **Check Purchase History**: Go to App Store > Account > Purchase History to verify the purchase
5. **Contact Support**: If still not working, email us with your purchase receipt

### Content Not Detecting Properly

**Problem**: Phone numbers, emails, or URLs aren't being detected

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Format**: Ensure the content is in a standard format (e.g., "email@example.com")
2. **Copy Clean Text**: Sometimes extra characters or formatting can interfere
3. **Manual Override**: You can still use the item even if it's not auto-detected
4. **Report**: Let us know about detection issues so we can improve

## Privacy & Security

### Data Storage
All clipboard data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. Your data is never transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (Pro feature).

### Permissions
MyClipVault requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:
- **Clipboard Access**: To save and retrieve your copied items
- **Contacts** (optional): For enhanced phone number and email detection
- **Calendar** (optional): To create events from detected dates

### No Tracking
We do not collect, track, or share your clipboard content. Your privacy is our top priority.
