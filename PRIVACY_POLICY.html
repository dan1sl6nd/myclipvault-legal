Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MyClipVault - Smart Clipboard ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Clipboard Content

  • Stored Locally: All clipboard items you save are stored locally on your device
  • Not Transmitted: We do NOT access, read, transmit, or store your clipboard content on our servers
  • Your Control: You have complete control over what clipboard items are saved
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device and is never synced to any cloud service

Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymized usage data:

  • Subscription status (weekly, yearly)
  • Purchase events (subscribe, renew, cancel)
  • Device type and OS version
  • App version

We do NOT collect:

  • Your clipboard content
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Device identifiers beyond what's required for subscriptions
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

Purchase Information

When you make a purchase:

  • Apple processes all payments - we never see your payment information
  • StoreKit 2 manages: Subscription status, transaction verification
  • We have access to: Purchase receipts, subscription status, subscription history
  • All subscription data is processed securely through Apple's infrastructure

Diagnostic Data

With your permission, we may collect:

  • Crash reports (through Apple's crash reporting)
  • Performance metrics
  • Error logs

This data is anonymized and used only to improve the App.

How We Use Your Information

Provide Services

  • Store and organize your clipboard history locally
  • Detect content types (URLs, emails, phone numbers, etc.)
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements

Improve the App

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on usage data
  • Optimize app performance

Process Subscriptions

  • Verify subscription status
  • Process payments through Apple
  • Provide customer support
  • Send subscription-related notifications (renewals, expirations)

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and safety

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Clipboard items are stored in a local CoreData database on your device
  • Data is protected by iOS security measures
  • Data is encrypted at rest using iOS built-in encryption
  • All data remains on your device only

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Apple (Payment Processing & Subscription Management)

  • Processes all payments through App Store
  • Manages subscriptions through StoreKit 2
  • Handles transaction verification and validation
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

No other third-party analytics or tracking services are used.

Data Retention

Clipboard Items

  • Stored indefinitely on your device (or until you delete them)
  • No automatic deletion (unlimited storage with subscription)
  • You can manually delete items at any time

Subscription Data

  • Retained as long as your subscription is active
  • Retained for up to 1 year after subscription ends (for customer support)
  • Anonymous transaction data may be retained longer for financial records

Account Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Uninstall the App from your device
  2. Contact us at dan1sl6nd@gmail.com to request deletion of subscription records

Your Rights

Access Your Data

  • View all clipboard items stored in the App
  • Request information about your subscription data

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual clipboard items
  • Clear all clipboard history
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data
  • Request deletion of subscription records

Control Data Collection

  • Disable analytics in Settings (if available)
  • Revoke clipboard access permissions (iOS Settings)

Opt-Out

  • Turn off usage analytics
  • Disable crash reporting
  • Unsubscribe from email communications

Children's Privacy

  • The App is not directed at children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us immediately
  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of their child's data

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at dan1sl6nd@gmail.com

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are an EU resident, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Contract: To provide subscription services
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve the App and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For optional analytics and marketing

Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • iOS built-in encryption for local storage
  • HTTPS/TLS for network communications with Apple's servers
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data collection to minimize risk

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: dan1sl6nd@gmail.com
Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

For privacy-related requests, please include:

  • Your Apple ID (if relevant)
  • Description of your request
  • Any relevant details to help us assist you

Summary

What we collect: Minimal usage analytics and subscription data

What we DON'T collect: Your clipboard content, personal information

Where data is stored: Locally on your device only

Who can access your data: Only you (we cannot access your clipboard content)

Your control: Full control to delete data anytime

We believe in privacy by design. Your clipboard content stays on your device.
